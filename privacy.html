Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 31, 2024

TL;DR: Agent Bridge doesn't collect, store, or transmit any of your data. Everything stays on your local WiFi network. We can't see your data because it never leaves your devices.

Introduction

Agent Bridge ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the Agent Bridge iOS app and macOS daemon handle your information.

Data Collection

Information We Don't Collect

Agent Bridge does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect any personally identifiable information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry data
  • We do not have access to your terminal sessions or AI agent data
  • We do not store any data on remote servers
  • We do not use third-party analytics or tracking services

How Agent Bridge Works

Local Network Only

Agent Bridge operates entirely on your local WiFi network:

  • The iOS app connects directly to the macOS daemon running on your Mac
  • All communication happens over your local network (LAN)
  • No data is sent to external servers or the internet
  • Terminal output and commands stay between your iPhone and Mac

Data Storage

The only data stored locally on your devices:

  • Pairing Credentials: Stored securely in the iOS Keychain to maintain connection with your Mac
  • Settings: Your notification preferences and other app settings stored locally using UserDefaults
  • Session History: Terminal output stored temporarily on your Mac during active sessions

Permissions

Camera Access

Used only to scan QR codes for pairing your iPhone with your Mac. Camera data is processed locally and not stored or transmitted.

Local Network Access

Required to communicate with the Agent Bridge daemon on your Mac. All communication is encrypted and stays on your WiFi network.

Notifications

Used to alert you when AI agents need input or finish tasks. You can disable this in Settings at any time.

Security

  • Pairing Tokens: Time-limited (60 seconds) and single-use for secure device pairing
  • Device Keys: Stored securely in the iOS Keychain and macOS Keychain
  • Local Only: No internet connection required or used for app functionality
  • Network Isolation: Works only on your local WiFi network

Children's Privacy

Agent Bridge does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app does not knowingly target children.

Third-Party Services

Agent Bridge does not integrate with or use any third-party services, analytics, or tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data about you, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over all data on your devices.
